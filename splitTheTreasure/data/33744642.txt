    <h2 class="cross-head">How did the new boys do?</h2>
    
          <p>    Watford have made 10 signings since winning promotion from the Championship, and Quique Sanchez Flores became the club&#039;s fifth manager in 12 months when replacing Slavisa Jokanovic in June.
</p>
  
          <p>    Six of those new players made their competitive debuts at Everton, but there was little indication of the Spaniard&#039;s team failing to gel.  
</p>
  
          <p>    Sebastian Prodl was composed at the back and made several key interceptions as the home side were frustrated for large periods of the game, while Etienne Capoue linked up well with Troy Deeney when the forward and captain dropped deep.
